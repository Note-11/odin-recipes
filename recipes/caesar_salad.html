Caesar Salad Recipe

Description

A classic Caesar salad with crisp romaine lettuce, creamy dressing, crunchy crout tons, and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the romaine lettuce. Chop it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the chopped lettuce with Caesar dressing until evenly coated.
  3. Add croutons and grated Parmesan cheese to the salad. Toss gently to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, serve with lemon wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh Caesar salad!
